About

From simulations to stoichiometry.

I earned my PhD in computational chemistry modeling molecules with code — then discovered the reaction I liked best happens in a classroom. For over twelve years I've taught high school chemistry, and for over eighteen I've been teaching in one form or another.

These days I split my energy between the lab bench and the keyboard: building games that make bonding intuitive, tools that replace clunky software, and systems that make a classroom hum. If it makes chemistry click for a student, it ships.
